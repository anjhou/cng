*Prices represent indicative current U.S. spot/futures trading ranges and may fluctuate intraday depending on regional supply-demand balances, refinery operations, export activity, and geopolitical developments.

  • OPEC+ production policy: Saudi Arabia and allied producers continue using voluntary production cuts to stabilize crude markets and defend price floors.
  • U.S. shale productivity: Permian Basin efficiency gains and associated gas/NGL production growth continue pressuring propane and domestic crude differentials.
  • Refinery maintenance cycles: Seasonal turnarounds across the U.S. Gulf Coast and Europe periodically tighten gasoline, diesel, and jet fuel supply balances.
  • Geopolitical disruptions: Risks involving the Strait of Hormuz, Red Sea shipping attacks, Russia sanctions, and Middle East instability continue adding risk premiums to crude and refined products.
  • IMO marine fuel regulations: Low-sulfur bunker fuel demand remains elevated because of international maritime sulfur compliance standards, supporting LSFO premiums over HSFO.
  • Petrochemical demand weakness: Slower manufacturing activity in China and Europe has reduced naphtha demand growth and compressed petrochemical margins.
  • Pipeline and logistics bottlenecks: Regional constraints in pipeline, storage, and export terminal infrastructure periodically widen regional price spreads between Gulf Coast, Midwest, and East Coast markets.
  • Capital investment trends: Investment continues flowing into LNG export terminals, NGL fractionation, refinery upgrades, and renewable diesel projects, reshaping long-term refined product balances.
  • Government regulations and energy transition: Carbon reduction mandates, renewable fuel standards (RFS), EV adoption policies, and low-carbon fuel standards are influencing gasoline and diesel demand expectations.
  • Trade imbalances and export dependence: The U.S. remains a major exporter of propane, diesel, and gasoline, making domestic pricing increasingly dependent on Latin American, European, and Asian demand conditions.
