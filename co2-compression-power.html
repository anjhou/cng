Interactive Pipeline Profile

Pipeline profile uses one point every 10 ft, equivalent to 10 data points per 100 ft. CO₂ compression is calculated as equal-ratio multistage adiabatic compression with intercooling back to the suction/intercooler temperature. This is a screening tool; dense-phase CO₂ and sequestration service require EOS-based real-gas properties, phase envelope checks, dehydration, metallurgy, compressor curves, and applicable pipeline codes.
