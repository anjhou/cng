CO₂ in Gas Stream

ṅ_CO2,in = Q_gas · y_CO2 · (P / (R · T))
R = 10.73 (psia·ft³)/(lb‑mol·°R)

CO₂ Captured

ṅ_captured = ṅ_CO2,in · η_capture

CO₂ Emitted

ṅ_emitted = ṅ_CO2,in − ṅ_captured

CO₂ Avoided

ṅ_avoided = ṅ_captured − ṅ_penalty
