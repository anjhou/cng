Fuel Quality Specifications

This page summarizes fuel quality requirements for refined transportation fuels: gasoline, ultra-low sulfur diesel (ULSD), and marine bunker fuel oils.

Gasoline (ASTM D4814 – U.S. Market)

Gasoline quality in the U.S. is governed primarily by ASTM D4814 and EPA Tier 3 regulations.

  • Octane (AKI): Regular 87, Mid‑grade 89–90, Premium 91–94.
  • Sulfur: Tier 3 gasoline sulfur cap of ~10 ppm average.
  • RVP: Seasonal limits.
  • Distillation: ASTM D86 limits.
  • Oxygenates: E10 or E15.

Ultra-Low Sulfur Diesel (ULSD – ASTM D975)

  • Sulfur: ≤ 15 ppm.
  • Cetane: ≥ 40.
  • Lubricity: HFRR ≤ 520 µm.
  • Cold flow: Seasonal.

Marine Bunker Fuel Oil (ISO 8217)

  • VLSFO: 0.5% sulfur.
  • HSFO: 3.5% sulfur (scrubbers).
  • MGO/MDO: Distillates.
