About Our Practice

Houston CNG Consulting provides high‑credibility chemical process technology expertise to clients across the energy, petrochemical, and specialty chemical sectors. Based in Houston, we support engineering teams, project developers, and asset owners with independent, technically rigorous insight.

Our practice combines deep process engineering experience with a pragmatic, operations‑focused mindset. We help clients improve reliability, reduce energy consumption, strengthen process safety, and evaluate investment decisions with clarity and confidence.
